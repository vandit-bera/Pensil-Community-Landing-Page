Frequently Asked Question

What is Pensil?

Pensil is a community-based learning and engaging platform. With Pensil, people can come together for shared interests, values, beliefs, or causes. Pensil keeps conversations organized by Groups You can host the discussion, files, and anything you want to share with your audience.

What is Community?

A community is a social unit with commonalities such as norms, religion, values, customs, or identity. Communities may share a sense of place situated in a given geographical area or in virtual space through communication platforms. People can come together for shared interests, values, beliefs, or causes.

How to create a New Group

A Group is a space where you can add multiple sections, depending on what you want to use these sections for.
- Open and Free For Everyone - Anyone with your community URL will be able to see the sections and content inside them.
- Closed and Free For Everyone - Users will be able to Request Access to this group. This Request needs to be approved by the Community Admin.
- Paid Group - Community Admin can decide a price tag for access to the group. Users have to first make payments to access such groups.

How to create a Post Section

A Posts type section is where you can put individual posts and other users can comment or upvote.
- This type of section is most beneficial for:
- Organizing important conversation topics Publishing content you want users to notice.

How to create a Chat Section

A Chat section is designed to enable real-time conversations between the community members.

How to upload PDF and other documents

Uploading any type of document on the Pensil communities is super simple:
- Click on Attachment Select the File you want to upload
- Click on Post button at the top Viola!
